Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Serves: 4 people

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g guanciale or pancetta, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Salt for pasta water

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry guanciale in a large pan until crispy, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated cheese, and plenty of black pepper.
  4. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain spaghetti.
  5. Remove pan from heat. Add spaghetti to guanciale and toss.
  6. Pour egg mixture over pasta and toss quickly, adding pasta water as needed.
  7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.
