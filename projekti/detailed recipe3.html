Ingredients

Ingredient 1 Salmon fillet
Ingredient 2 Butter
Ingredient 3 Garlic
Ingredient 4 Honey
Ingredient 5 Soy sauce
Ingredient 6 Lemon

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Season and sear Salmon.
  2. Step 2: Add butter, garlic, honey, soy.
  3. Step 3: Cook until sticky.
  4. Step 4: Finish with lemon

Tips

  • Cook salmon skin-side down first for crispness.
  • Remove from heat early - salmon continues cooking.
  • Lemon at the end makes the honey glaze brighter.
